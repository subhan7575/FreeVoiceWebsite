About Free Voice

Free Voice is a realistic voice changer app that allows users to record, upload, and transform their voice into human-like voices. The app supports offline usage for downloaded voices and premium live voice changing features.

Developer & Technology

Developer

Subhan Ahmad – The creator and owner of Free Voice, responsible for design, development, and updates.

AI-Powered Voice Conversion

Free Voice uses advanced AI models to convert and modify voices into realistic human-like speech. Premium voices offer ultra-realistic options for live and recorded use.

Updates & Maintenance

New voices and features are automatically updated across all platforms. Offline voices remain available after download, and premium live voices require active membership.
