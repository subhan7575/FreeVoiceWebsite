Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Free Voice collects minimal information and protects user data while providing voice changing services.

Data Collection & Usage

Free Voice uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users. We do not store passwords or share personal email information.

Voice Recordings

Recorded and uploaded voices are temporarily processed for conversion. Offline downloaded voices are stored locally on the user device. No uploaded or converted voice files are shared with third parties.

Premium Membership

Membership status, subscription type, and remaining time are stored in Firebase securely. Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe and are not stored on our servers.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies or analytics to improve website performance. No personal data is tracked or sold.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please contact us using the contact page. Your email is not stored or displayed publicly.
